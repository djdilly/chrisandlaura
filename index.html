How do I get there?

Planes

Essex is very well served by all London's major airports. Although the closest airport is London Stansted. There will be regular buses to Chelmsford directly from the Airport. To fly from Northern Ireland, Chris recommends flying from Belfast International to London Stansted using Easyjet. There are several flights daily to suit everyones schedule.

Trains

Witham has an excellent train connection from London Liverpool Street or London Stratford Stations. This service runs very regularly, and takes between 50 minutes and 60 minutes. Don't be late though...Laura wo n't wait on you.

WARNING Irish folk,

Stand on the right of the escalators...Londoner's can be grouchy...

and automobiles

Witham is on the A12, directly between Colchester and Chelmsford. So..drive to one of those Cities, head for the other, via the A12, and stop half way...simples...or there are plenty of GPS apps and devices about...
