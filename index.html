How do I get there?

Planes

Essex is very well served by all London's major airports although the closest airport is London Stansted. There will be regular buses to Chelmsford directly from the Airport. To fly from Northern Ireland, Chris recommends flying from Belfast International to London Stansted using Easyjet. There are several flights daily to suit everyone's schedule.


Trains

Chelmsford and Witham have an excellent train connection from London Liverpool Street or London Stratford Stations. This service runs very regularly, and takes between 40 minutes and 50 minutes.
